Rookie Bounty Hunter

Bounty Hunter is already in a good spot and I'm not aware of it needing any major changes.

However, and I talk about this in my video, what if there were a risk-free version of Bounty Hunter?

And maybe you reduce the rewards and EP by 50% or even 90% or you can't buy the monetary value rewards to compensate.

And in this version of BH you can only get a "Rookie Bounty Hunter Hat" instead of the real one.

This is not my highest priority suggestion, but I was thinking about it and I realized that it would take out a lot of the BS for a player like me who dislikes risk PKing.

It also would address problems I mentioned of skill gap, content gap, and how "bronze players don't have a place to fight other bronze players".

This could be the perfect low-stakes safe PvP minigame for players to try out PvP.

One of my critiques of the PvP Arena is the downtime - you have to go back to the lobby and requeue to find another fight and it just takes extra time and takes you out of it.

In the Bounty Hunter island, it's just an area full of people looking for a fight. While the area matches a target for you, you could just go rogue and fight whoever as people are just waiting around.

That actually sounds great to me, if only it weren't risk PKing.

If they were to make 1 world dedicated to a safe version of Bounty Hunter, then…

  1. You could easily find fights fast and have fun doing PvP.
  2. You wouldn't need to spend any time re-gearing every time you die.
  3. There would be no problem with rushers, because you aren't killing people for their loot, making less incentive for people to try rushing.
  4. Even if they do rush, you don't lose anything, so it doesn't matter if you die that way.
  5. Plus, there would be no better way for you to learn how to fight in Bounty Hunter, than by actually doing a safe version of Bounty Hunter.

The consequence is that it would be dominated by high level players using max gear, because there would be zero gear risk to doing that.

…

Which would not be a problem if we had Minigame Hybrid Armor and Minigame Weapons for less rich players to be able to compete.

Or alternatively there could be a gear-limit where you can't equip gear that's level 80 or above. Lots of PvPers often suggest ideas like that and it could be a good way to level the playing field for less rich players.

I don't foresee this taking too many players away from regular risk-based Bounty Hunter, because the players already engaging in that are already okay with risk. They would stay in risk Bounty Hunter because the rewards are also gained twice as fast or ten times as fast.

A safe version of Bounty Hunter would most likely attract just a completely different crowd of players.

Plus, if players find that they can do well in this version of Bounty Hunter, there may be a good chance that they just graduate to real risk Bounty Hunter.
