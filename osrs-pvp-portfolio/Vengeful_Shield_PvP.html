Vengeful Shield

It's a shield that has a "special defense" mechanic (similar to the Raids 4 Rondache Shield or Dragonfire Shield).

It gives you a weaker version of Vengeance, which will deflect 45% of the damage you take back to your attacker. Compare to the 75% from Vengeance.

It shares a cooldown with Vengeance. You cannot use both at the same time.

If you cast Vengeance after activating the shield's ability, Vengeance will just override it being the stronger version.

It requires 40 defense to equip. Note that Vengeance also requires 40 defense to unlock.

Alternatively, it could require 1 defense and 40 hitpoints to equip, or 1 defense and 40 magic.

It has minimal defensive stats and no offensive bonuses. The low defense is because it's meant to be used for the Vengeance effect, so you want to get hit.

So what's the point of this shield?

It enables "Venge-PKing" for a much much wider range of accounts and players than currently can engage with it.

Venge-PKing is locked behind 94 magic. In other words, you have to nearly max out a stat to be able to try it.

It makes no sense for such a popular style of PvP to be locked away from newer lower level players.

This item should offer a lot of bang for its buck. It opens up a LOT more PvP potential for more players.
