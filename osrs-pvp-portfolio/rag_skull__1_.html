Rag Skull
Ragged Status — Wilderness Anti-Rag Mechanic
What Is Ragging
Ragging is repeatedly attacking someone with minimal risk — often 1-iteming with a powerful spec weapon hoping for a lucky kill, or an entire clan blocking an area for free. It costs the ragger nothing. It wastes everyone else’s time and supplies.

Rag Skull addresses this through game mechanics, not manual bans. It enforces a minimum risk on any player who initiates attack in the Wilderness.
How It Works
Trigger Condition
You enter the Wilderness with less than the minimum risk threshold. Upon initiating attack on another player — in any situation where you would normally receive a skull — you receive a Rag Skull instead.
Rag Skull Rules
Acts as a high-risk skull. You cannot use Protect Item prayer. You drop everything on death. It looks like a regular skull with distinct brown rags wrapped around it, like a mummy.
↺
Automatic Removal
Instantly downgraded or removed when you no longer meet the criteria — pick up a high-value drop that raises your risk, re-enter with more gear, or leave the Wilderness entirely.
⚠
Player Warnings
A warning message box appears before entering the Wilderness if you would be Rag Skulled. A UI indicator also shows whether attacking a specific player would trigger Rag Skull status.

Risk Threshold — Two Versions
Version 1
Flat 100k Minimum Risk
Simple floor. If your potential loot key value (skulled with Protect Item) is below 100k, you are Rag Skulled on attack.
Voidwaker (~40m): Requires 100k risk minimum. A clear threshold — though 100k may still feel cheap for powerful specs.
Version 2 — Preferred
Percentage of Weapon Value
Risk floor scales with how powerful your gear is. Fairer and harder to exploit at any price point.
At 5%: Voidwaker → 2m risk. Zaryte Crossbow (~350m) → 17.5m risk.
At 1%: Voidwaker → 400k. ZCB → 3.5m. Intuitive — divide price by 100.
Risk is calculated from the estimated loot key value — simulating the worst case as if you were skulled with Protect Item active. Jagex picks the exact percentage that feels right in practice.
