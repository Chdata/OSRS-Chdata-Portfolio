Community Ranking Poll · 2026

PvP Update Priority Ranking

Rank the PvP updates you most want to see developed. Your #1 choice is the update you want most. The community's top-ranked update will be prioritised for development.

How This Poll Works

1.
Drag or use the arrows to rank the listed updates from most wanted (#1) to least wanted (#10).
2.
The update ranked #1 by the most players becomes the community's chosen priority.
3.
Jagex commits to developing the top-ranked update, subject to a standard in-game poll before release.
4.
All other updates remain in future ranking polls — nothing is ruled out permanently.
🗓
Ranking closes 28 July 2026 You must be a member to participate. One submission per account.

Rank PvP Updates by Priority

Drag to reorder or use the ▲▼ arrows. Place the update you want developed most at position #1. Updates are split by development size — please rank each category separately.
Update Size Definitions:

■ Small — Quality of Life updates that realistically only take a week or two to develop.
■ Medium — Updates that might take 1–3 months to develop.
■ Large — Updates that might take 4–6 months to develop.
■ Massive — A raid-sized update that will take 6+ months and significant effort and resources. Similar to the effort put in for Raids and Sailing.
"No Vote" Options:

If any of the No Vote options make it to rank #1, they will be skipped for the next highest ranking PvP update.
