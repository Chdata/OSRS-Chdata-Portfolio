About This Page
How to Use This Reference
This page compiles every polled question related to PvP or the Wilderness in Old School RuneScape from 2013 through 2026, sourced directly from the official OSRS website and the OSRS Wiki Polls archive. Question wording, answer options, and percentage results are reproduced verbatim. The passing threshold was 75% from launch until 27 October 2022, when Jagex reduced it to 70% (Polling Changes blog). Each question displays its applicable threshold. Below the poll list is a separate table of significant unpolled changes to PvP and the Wilderness.
